## Organization Overview

The core offering: take the AIOS built and proven by the founder on his own agency, and replicate it inside client businesses to eliminate operational overhead and automate recurring tasks.

- **Primary early targets:** Small hospitals, medical/healthcare practices, and small businesses

- **No niche locked yet** — will sharpen based on which early clients produce the best outcomes

## Products / Services

- **AIOS Installation** — Full setup of an AI Operating System inside a client's business using Claude Code; the founder's own workspace serves as the proven template
- **Business Process Automation** — Identifying and automating recurring client tasks (admin, reporting, communication, scheduling)
- **Ongoing AI Strategy** — Guidance on how to expand automation as the client's AIOS matures
