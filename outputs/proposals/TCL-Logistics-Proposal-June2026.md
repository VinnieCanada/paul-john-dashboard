## What We're Building Together

### Module 1 — Consignment Automation
**The problem it solves:** Your team spends 3–4 hours every day manually entering consignment data. That's time they're not spending on anything that actually grows the business.

**What this does:**
- A WhatsApp number your team already uses becomes the intake point — they type or voice-note the consignment details the same way they'd send a message
- Our system reads it, validates it, and enters it into Fleetx automatically — no manual keying
- If something's missing or unclear, it asks for it before logging the entry
- At the end of every day, a clean summary report goes to you showing everything logged, flagged, and pending
- Handles 250+ entries a day without breaking a sweat

**What changes for you:** Your team stops doing data entry. Entirely. Those 3–4 hours a day go back to them.

---

### Module 2 — Email Automation
**The problem it solves:** Your clients expect updates. Right now someone on your team is manually sending those — or not sending them, and getting calls instead.

**What this does:**
- The moment a consignment is booked, your client automatically receives a confirmation email with the details and expected delivery window
- When the truck is dispatched, they get a dispatch notification with the driver's name and estimated arrival
- If there's a delay or a route change, they're notified before they even think to call
- When the delivery is complete, a proof-of-delivery confirmation goes out automatically
- Every morning at 8 AM, you get a summary of the day's deliveries, delays, and fleet status — without asking for it

**What changes for you:** Client calls asking "where is my shipment?" drop significantly. Your team isn't chasing updates — the system handles it. You look more professional to every client, every time.

---

### Module 3 — TCL Operations Dashboard
**The problem it solves:** Right now, knowing what's happening in your business means calling drivers, checking Fleetx manually, asking your team, and piecing it together. There's no single place where the full picture lives.

**What this does — Fleet Tracking:**
- Every truck in your fleet shows up live on the dashboard with its current location, speed, status (moving / stopped / delayed), the driver's name, what it's carrying, and its ETA
- One click opens Google Maps showing exactly where the vehicle is right now
- Delayed trucks are flagged automatically — you see it before a client calls about it
- No more calling drivers to ask "where are you?" — the answer is already on the screen

**What this does — Operations View:**
- Active consignments, their status, and which truck is carrying them — all in one place
- Daily and weekly KPI summary: deliveries completed, in transit, delayed, and pending
- A printable morning report generated automatically — ready before your day starts
- All email automation activity visible: what was sent, to whom, and when

**What changes for you:** You open one screen and know exactly what's happening in your business. Not after a round of phone calls — immediately, from anywhere, including your phone.
