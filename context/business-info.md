## Organization Overview

**answr.in** — a one-person AI consultancy that installs AI Operating Systems (AIOS) for small businesses and healthcare practices in Coorg and beyond. The core offering: take the AIOS built and proven by the founder on his own agency, and replicate it inside client businesses to eliminate operational overhead and automate recurring tasks. Positioned as Coorg's dedicated AI partner.

## Target Clients

- **Primary early targets:** Coorg-based businesses — coffee estates, homestays/hotels, healthcare practices, logistics, and local small businesses
- **Geographic focus:** Coorg (Kodagu), Karnataka — tight-knit business community where referrals travel fast
- **Why Coorg:** Underserved by AI consultants, strong local business network, clear ROI for high-admin industries like hospitality, agriculture, and healthcare

## Products / Services

- **AIOS Installation** — Full setup of an AI Operating System inside a client's business using Claude Code; the founder's own workspace serves as the proven template
- **Business Process Automation** — Identifying and automating recurring client tasks (admin, reporting, communication, scheduling)
- **Ongoing AI Strategy** — Guidance on how to expand automation as the client's AIOS matures

## Business Model

- **Stage:** Pre-revenue, first clients being acquired
- **Client acquisition:** Warm outreach to existing network, then referrals as the primary growth engine
- **Pricing:** Not yet defined — open decision. Early clients likely on a founding/discounted rate while the engagement model is refined
- **Engagement model:** Project-based installation (scope, timeline, and deliverables still being standardized)

## Key Context

- Solo founder / operator — no team, no contractors yet
- The founder's own AIOS workspace is the product being demonstrated and replicated
- Primary tooling: Claude Code (this workspace is home base for both agency operations and client delivery)
