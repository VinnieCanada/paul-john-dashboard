## Current Focus Period

Next 30–60 days (May–June 2026)

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Nail one client before scaling** — land the first client (hospital or small business via warm outreach) and make the result undeniably good. One great outcome is worth more than four mediocre ones at this stage.
2. **Define the standard engagement** — lock down: what does a client get, how long does it take, and what does it cost? Even a rough answer makes outreach confident and sales repeatable.
3. **Set a price before the first conversation** — commit to a founding client rate (likely a flat setup fee) before approaching prospects. Removes the negotiation friction.
4. **Earn referrals through quality** — healthcare referral networks are tight. One great hospital client can unlock several more.

## 6-Month Target

- **4 AIOS installs per month** by month 6 (~24 businesses total)
- Hospitals and small businesses as the primary client base
- Referrals as the primary growth engine by month 3–4

## What Success Looks Like (30–60 days)

- First client onboarded and happy with the result
- Standard engagement scoped: clear deliverables, timeline, and price
- At least one referral conversation initiated
- AIOS install process feels repeatable, not improvised

## Key Decisions or Open Questions

- **Pricing model:** Flat setup fee vs. monthly retainer vs. hybrid — decide before first sales conversation
- **Standard engagement:** What exactly does a client get? How many hours? What modules? What's included vs. out of scope?
- **Niche focus:** Hospitals are the early bet — worth doubling down if first engagement goes well
